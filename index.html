Sports, E-sports & IP-Themed Gaming Provider

Next-generation provably fair games for iGaming platforms, featuring authentic sports, e-sports and IP themed partnerships utilizing blockchain technology.

Concept Overview

ArenaDrop is a next-generation gaming provider specializing in sports, e-sports, and intellectual property themed games for iGaming platforms. We create provably fair blockchain-based games that integrates with Brazilian and international iGaming platforms, offering unique themed gaming experiences across multiple entertainment verticals.

Gaming Provider Model
Integration Platform: Our games will integrate seamlessly into existing iGaming platforms through standard APIs, providing themed iGaming content that will leverage popular sports, e-sports, and entertainment intellectual properties with complete transparency and fairness.
Target iGaming Partners: Brazilian and international licensed iGaming operators seeking differentiated iGaming content with authentic sports, e-sports and IP themes.
Proprietary Provably Fair Technology: As pioneers in this technology, all games will operate with 100% transparency using our proprietary blockchain verification system on Lisk Layer 2, ensuring complete fairness and building trust between operators and players.

Target Market

Our target market will consist of strategic partners and engaged end users who will be aligned with our gaming provider model and sports-themed entertainment offerings.

Primary Partners

Licensed iGaming Operators: Established platforms seeking sports-themed content to differentiate their gaming offerings and engage sports-passionate audiences.
Sports Organizations: Football clubs, e-sports teams, sports leagues and IP holders seeking digital engagement opportunities and new revenue streams through authentic gaming partnerships.
IP Holders: Sports federations, gaming companies, and entertainment properties looking to monetize their intellectual property through innovative gaming experiences.

End User Demographics

Sports iGaming Enthusiasts: Regular iGaming players with strong interests in sports who value authentic themed experiences and transparent, fair gameplay.
E-sports Fans: Gamers engaged with competitive gaming, particularly interested in CS:GO, League of Legends, and other popular e-sports titles.
Sports Collectors: Users interested in sports memorabilia and exclusive items who appreciate the potential for physical rewards and authentic experiences.
